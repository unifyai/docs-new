In some cases, deployment locally on your own virtual private cloud (VPC) or on your own servers is important,
especially in cases where privacy is of utmost importance. The
[Universal API](https://docs.unify.ai/universal_api),
[Benchmarking](https://docs.unify.ai/benchmarking) and
[Routing](https://docs.unify.ai/routing)
can *all* be deployed in a fully on-prem manner.

To get access to each of these services for on-prem deployments,
simply [book a call](https://calendly.com/unify-chat/general) with us!
